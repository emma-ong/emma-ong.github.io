About me

Why did I join EDA?


My background has since been in health after graduating university. Ironically, being in the health sector revived my interest in web developement, something that I dabbled in once upon a time ago as a kid.
I got back on track with code, and during the timeline of re-exploring this interest, a friend who knew a few somebodies that attended EDA, provided glowing reviews about the course.
With this, along with my motivation to accelerate my learning journey, I finally built up the courage to pursue web development as a career and see EDA as an opportunity to facilitate this goal.
